Systems Programming Ghent

A community for systems programmers to share a passion for performant and reliable software.

What We Do

We aim to bring code enthusiasts together by organising events in Ghent and the surrounding area:

  • Presentations: Topics include (but are not limited to) compiled programming languages such as Rust, Haskell or C++.
  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions to learn new programming or engineering skills and tools from people acive in the field.
  • Socials: Informal gatherings such as dinners or drinks to meet and chat with like-minded people.

To register for an event, please send an e-mail to sysghent@proton.me.

For Whom

We are searching for developers passionate about performance optimization, security, and formal methods in software development. This could be, for example, hobbyists or professionals active in fields such as:

  • Embedded systems
  • Operating systems
  • Compilers
  • Networking
  • Security

Why We Do It

While the open-source community online is very rich and friendly, people might prefer to meet in-person. Currently, there are not that many groups in Ghent targeted towards back-end or systems developers.

Who We Are

This community was started by a software engineer at OTIV in Ghent (Belgium), a company specialized in software for operating railway vehicles.

This community is still fresh and in need of volunteers. Please reach out by mail at sysghent@proton.me if you would like to help.
